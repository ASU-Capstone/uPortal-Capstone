Requirements:

  • JDK 1.7 or later
  • Tomcat 7.x or later is required. (Servlet 3.0 is required.) Other servlet containers may work. YMMV.
  • Database (Oracle, Postgres, MySQL are commonly used in production; these and HSQLDB are commonly used in development; specific vendors and versions are supported via declaration in the rdbm.properties file)
